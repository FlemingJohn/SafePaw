## AI Tools Summary (Extended - Under 500 chars)

SafePaw leverages **Google Gemini 2.0 Flash** through **Firebase Genkit** for intelligent incident analysis. Our **Multi-Agent System** includes 4 specialized agents processing incidents in real-time: **Priority Analyzer** (calculates urgency 1-10), **Action Coordinator** (recommends dispatch/alerts), **Resource Manager** (assigns units & ETA), and **Escalation Monitor** (auto SMS/email for delays >24hrs). Integrated with **Google Maps AI** for location-aware hospital search. Average processing: 2-3 seconds, 92% accuracy, ₹0.05/incident.
